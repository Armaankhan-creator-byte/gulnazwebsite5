DREAM WORLD ARCHITECTS

Incorported in 2014, ASRO Arcade is a Gurgaon based leading Architecture and Urban Design Firm focused on creating distinctive spaces and interiors. We provide end-to-end architecture; designing and interior solutions with operations spread across Delhi-NCR, Goa and Maharashtra.

Our specialization lies in residential buildings, apartments, Farm houses, Hospitality, Schools, Workplace Design, and Retail Design.

Our focus is to provide maximum value to our clients by delivering high quality projects through premium designs, expert knowledge & service excellence. We offer a range of strategies and a powerful implementation plan with the methodology to succeed in a given framework of time, quality and cost.

​

It is our continuous commitment to value client’s interest and work towards creating great human experiences. Our vision is to create architectural buildings and spaces that work in synchronicity with their surroundings and have an ‘inviting Aura’.
